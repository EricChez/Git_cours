Flex box

flex-direction: row; ---> will align the divs in a row

flex-direction: row-reverse; ---> will align the divs in reverse order

flex-direction: column; ---> will align the divs in columns

flex-direction: column-reverse; ---> will align the divs in reverse order

flex-direction: row; ---> will align the divs in a row and will adjust the width automatically

flex-direction: row;
flex-wrap: wrap; ---> will return to a new line when the aligned divs exceed the container's width

flex: 1; flex: 2; flex: 5; ---> will give a width to the div relative to that given to the other divs

justify-content: center; ---> will center the divs (in a row here)
justify-content acts on the main axis, hence we need to be mindful of the direction adopted

justify-content: space-evenly; ---> will center the divs and space them evenly (in a row here)

justify-content: space-around; ---> will center the divs with space around one another (in a row here)

justify-content: space-between; ---> will center the divs with space between them (in a row here)

flex-direction: row;
align-items: center; ---> align-items will align the div along the secondary axis (here, vertical center)

flex direction: column;
align-items: center; ---> align-items will align the div along the secondary axis (here, horizontal center)
